Facilities

  1. Library

    Library is the Heart of an Institute. The Teaching and Learning System are supported by the library through reading materials. Hence with a good intention it was started in the year 2010. Our library is well equipped with reference books, Text books and subscribes to various Journals and e- resources related to Commerce and Management and other subjects. More than 6000+ e journals 1,99,500+ e books can be accessed under The Project entitled “National Libraryand Information Services Infrastructure for Scholarly Content (N-LIST)” of INFLIBNET Centre, Ahmedabad. Library building is situated in the main building of the Institute. The total area of the library is 965.0 Sq.fts.
